WELCOME TO THE WESTGARDENS
Nestled in the County Kildare village of Sallins, the Westgardens Hotel is a mere 18 minutes from the M5 and Maynooth and the M7 and Naas. The Westgardens Hotel is perfectly located to tour Irelands Ancient East. Easily accessible to all local Kildare amenities such as Punchestown, Mondello, Kildare Village Outlet Centre and the Curragh to name but a few, the 5 star Westgrove Hotel provides the perfect haven for the leisure or business traveller when staying in Kildare. If you are looking for a family break, our Family Apartments offer the perfect home away from home and there are some fantastic family attractions nearby - Bargetrip.ie, Clonfert Pet Farm, Wallaby Woods Donadea, Lullymore Heritage and Discovery Park and lots, lots more.
